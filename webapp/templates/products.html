CovidNearMe.org

CovidNearMe.org is a crowdsourcing platform that allows individuals to provide information about the COVID-19 pandemic in their area. COVIDNearMe allows users to share information about hospital capacity and needed supplies, local COVID-19 cases and recoveries, and to input symptoms to help identify new outbreaks. The platform also allows users to view trends in their community or to comment on a hospital experience. To learn more, and to input data, please visit covidnearme.org.
