About Us

We are the “Data Science Response Team” (DSRT) an all-volunteer coalition of data scientists, research scientists, physicians, and technologists from academia, industry, and the US government. The DSRT provides a whole-of-society approach to the fight against COVID-19 by leveraging quantitative analysis that provides better insights for policy makers so that more lives may be saved.

We have created “Cadeus Covid” platform as an open-source, no-cost data science resource hub for policymakers, hospital administrators, and government officials at all levels to respond to the COVID-19 Pandemic. Specifically, Caedus Covid exists to enable key decision makers in government and healthcare to make data-driven policy and operational decisions based on accurate and localized predictions surrounding caseloads and resources.

DSRT, via the Caedus Covid platform, seeks to collaborate and coordinate with any and all existing efforts to help equip public health and emergency management decision makers with the very best:

1. Community-level outbreak modeling

2. Hospital supply and resource modeling to include beds and ventilators
