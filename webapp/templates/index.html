Cadeus Covid is an open-source, public-interest data science resource hub for policymakers, hospital administrators, and government officials at all levels to respond to the COVID-19 Pandemic.

Caedus Covid exists to enable key decision makers in government and healthcare to make data-driven policy and operational decisions based on accurate and localized predictions surrounding caseloads and resources. Our mission is to identify or create the very best data model and make it available to all decision-makers.
